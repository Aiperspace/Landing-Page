Engineering time is wasted on documentation.

Not because teams lack discipline, but because systems are fragmented, manual, and impossible to keep in sync.

  • Manual technical reporting The same facts re-entered across reports, reviews, and release packages.
  • Alignment across documents ICDs, specs, and test evidence drift apart; reconciliation waits until someone notices.
  • Version and history Tracing what changed and why means opening three tools and a folder nobody labeled.
  • Finding the signal Engineers search instead of deciding; outdated templates and siloed data compound the cost.

Our mission

Make satellite documentation autonomous.

Not hands-off engineering: self-sustaining baselines where repetition, inconsistency, and manual trace-upkeep are systematically removed. Judgment stays human; busywork does not.

Platform

One workspace from template to controlled export

Structured inputs feed ECSS-aware drafts. Compatibility and dependencies stay visible. Revisions and trace artifacts stay tied to the baseline your program actually runs.
