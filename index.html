FAQ

Short answers. Deep details in the demo.

Do you support ECSS tailoring and NASA compliance?

Yes. We support EARM, ECM, RTM, and VCD as living artifacts. DRD-aware generation and proactive conflict detection keep you audit-ready.

Will we be locked into your platform or format?

No. Open APIs and export in open formats. Integrations with tools like DOORS, Jira are on our roadmap; your data stays yours. No proprietary lock-in.

How does AI work—and who validates it?

Every AI suggestion is human-in-the-loop. We provide explainable rationale (XAI) so engineers can verify, not trust a black box. Traceability is 100% with expert sign-off.

Where is our data stored? What about ITAR and IP?

AWS GovCloud or Azure Government; regional residency and data sovereignty options. On-prem for sensitive missions. Local LLM option so proprietary data never leaves your environment; we do not train on your content without explicit opt-in.

Can we run on-prem?

Yes. Cloud, VPC, or on-prem to meet program and export-control constraints.

How do pilots work? Do we have to rip and replace?

No. Crawl-walk-run: we start with non-critical subsystems, connect to your existing tools, and measure engineering hours saved and EIDP time. 12-week pilots with Super Users and role-specific training.
